Frequently Asked Questions

Before you order

Does every order include the 100% Account Protection Guarantee?

Yes. The guarantee is included with Cat Food, Specific Units, VIP Deals, and Stage Progression orders. We document the account before processing, provide service updates, and verify the result after completion. Coverage applies to verified issues caused during our service process.

How do customers order from the website?

Every Cat Food, Specific Unit, VIP Deal, and Stage Progression offer includes a direct WhatsApp order button. The selected package information is already written automatically in the message.

What payments are shown on the website now?

The website now shows crypto icons for BTC, ETH, SOL, and USDT, plus QRIS as an additional payment icon for offers that support it.

How do Crown Completion discounts work?

Each crown is an individual completion with the same normal price for its campaign. Stories of Legend, Uncanny Legends, and Zero Legends apply 15% off to 2-Crown, 20% off to 3-Crown, and 25% off to 4-Crown. Availability and scope are confirmed before payment.

Can the customer order VIP Deals and Stage Progression from the website too?

Yes. Dedicated sections for VIP Deals and Stage Progression were added, each with its own WhatsApp order action.

Are the Cat Food bonuses cumulative?

Yes. Bonus thresholds are based on the total Cat Food purchased in one order and are confirmed again in WhatsApp before payment.
